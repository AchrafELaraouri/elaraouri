During my 2021 internship at Fruit of the Loom, I gained valuable experience in industrial sewing machine repair and maintenance. The internship enhanced my technical skills, instilled a sense of responsibility, efficiency, and teamwork. I am grateful for the opportunity to have worked with such a reputable company and gained valuable insights into the textile industry.
